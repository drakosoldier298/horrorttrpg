## Description
5'8" tall Humanoid figure made of what seems to be smoke. 

No details can be seen from its face. 

### Abilities
 This Anomaly has multiple abilities that cause it to be quite dangerous.
**Phasing** 
 - Allows the anomaly to walk through walls, Grab people and pull them through walls.

#### Interactions

Interactions with the entity are sparse. 

It has been spotted around towns and facilities stalking people.
Its been observed leaving people and animals partially inside objects, which kills them almost immediately with the only way to save them by using other anomalies.


Staff have been able to talk it out of fighting other entities, It however refuses to be convinced to leave its prey alone. 

If you think you are a target Stay in a group. It does not like groups.
