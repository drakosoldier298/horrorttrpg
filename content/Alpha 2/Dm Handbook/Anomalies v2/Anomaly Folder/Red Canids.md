This is a V2 of "The red dogs" Which was a direct creation of scp-939
This Anomaly shares many similarities with 939. 


# Description
This description is not exhaustive, 

These creatures are quadrupeds, Meaning they walk on 4 legs, they range from the size of a malamute to the size of a SUV. 

They have Slick Red skin as if made of faux leather, When cut the skin seems to tear and fray like fabric instead of skin. The blood is a deep black, Oil in consistency and Highly flammable, Because of this they seem to highly fear fire.

They cannot see unless you are 5 feet in front of them otherwise they emit a constant slight hissing sound, akin to television static, this acts like echolocation of sorts which allows them to "see" great distances
## Personality
These creatures are pack creatures, rarely seen alone.

They are very curious creatures and will approach with great speed, This has terrified multiple researchers and Security personal.

**Facility notes**
Any staff trying to tame one of these creatures will be demoted or moved from researching.
