### Description
A 6'2 Masculine shadow.

This anomaly has not been seen very often, so most of the document is incomplete.


#### Interactions
The Anomaly has been Spotted stalking people, Moving around them in a Slow and "elegant" way, Floating. 

Those who get close enough to spot it When it is stalking another person swear they saw a large smile on the "back" of its head. 

These people also claim to hear whispering all around them getting louder and more clear the closer they got to the person. 

The few victims that were able to be interviewed give wildly different accounts of what the Anomaly is.

Some Said it was an Old friend they had lost, Others said it was a demon sent there to get them to hell. 

The only common ground between the accounts is that the victims of the Anomaly Slowly go insane and acting off.

The Facility does not know what the Anomaly is.

- - - 


### Mechanics

The Anomaly will stalk a victim using any method it can to gain its trust, Gaining the victims trust is not required.

After gaining the trust or enough time has passed the Anomaly will Consume the victims mind and act as the body's host, Mimicking their behavior as close as it can and try to infect others.
