# Description

Tempest is a rock the size of a softball, 

The exact description varies as it changes depending on who touched it last, influenced by their emotions.

One researcher found it atop a mountain in the middle of a blazing heatwave, it was shooting out harmless red lightning with cracks of what appeared to be fire, the heatwave stopped the moment it was touched and the researcher was able to pocket it for the <span style="background:#000000"><font color="#000000">Facility</font></span> To retrieve it.
 
Eventually it was lost again and reobtained under the ocean emitting a thunderstorm, This was particularly difficult to obtain as any drone sent to retrieve it was destroyed by lightning.

upon letting go or leaving a 5m vicinity of the last user who touched it it will start emitting some natural weather event.
