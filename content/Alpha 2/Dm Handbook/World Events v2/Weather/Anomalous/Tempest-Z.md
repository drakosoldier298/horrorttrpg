Tempest-Z is another variant of tempest, it is the size of a basketball and seems to emit apocalyptic events. 

# Tempest-Z is to be held in an emp field containment at all times, at no point are any personnel allowed to interact with it

## Events witnessed by personnel
During our first interaction with Tempest-Z the sky turned red and it started raining a red substance similar to blood, all who were caught outside of shade had their skin immediately start to melt off in chunks, through their clothing and even any protective suits.

It took several days to retrieve it, At the time we did not know how to contain it. So it did not take long for an entire Site to be taken over by another event.
