# Description
Tempest-A is a variant of tempest.

It is the size of a marble and emits anomalous weather events, 

During first retrieval it was found in an apartment that started flooding with blood after being placed in a "marble flower pot" 

The person who owned the apartment tried to call the police and was arrested, Nothing is else is known about them.

Upon second retrieval it was found in a store on a mountain top, not long after the store had received it, the area started having acid rain, Not acidic enough to cause severe damage but enough to irritate any skin it came into contact with
