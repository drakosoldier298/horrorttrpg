#### Core Attributes
These are the attributes that all Entities have.
They have a minimum of -10 and a max of 20

All characters start with 5 and roll a d10 
if you roll a 10 you add 5 gaining a 10 in the attribute
If you roll a 1 You do -4 gaining a 1 in the attribute.

You cannot get above a 10 Without level ups or Going into the negatives.

You can choose to lower an attribute into the negatives to increase an attribute for the same amount. 
You can not go below -5 Without input from an Anomaly
Example If you lower Strength to -5 you can then increase an attribute by 5 

5 Grants a +0 to your attribute rolls

10 is a +5 To attribute rolls

15 is a +10


**Strength** - Character performing Feats of strength, Ex: Ripping a door off its hinges, Kicking a door in, Pulling open an elevator door, Breaking a lock open.

**Dexterity** - Character Performing Feats of Dexterity Ex: Running down multiple hallways without tripping, Travelling between towns/Safe Zones.
 
**Intelligence** - Character performing Feats of Intelligence. Ex: hacking a electronic lock, Finding Specific Knowledge in A Book Or Anomaly entry that others wouldn't.

**Communication** - Character Being good with communication. Ex: Convincing a trader to sell them goods for cheaper, Convincing an NPC to give them information about an anomaly or The location of a town/safepoint. Calming down NPC's, PC's, Anomalies during conflict

**Energy** - Determines the amount of actions A player can take during one turn of combat.
If you do not expend all energy it refreshes at half + What you had left over next turn, This cannot exceed your base energy.

**Sanity** - Works different from other Core Attributes and will get a more in depth explanation on [its own page]([[Sanity and anxiety v2]])

Determines how many situations that raise anxiety you can endure before negative affects begin. Restores on a long Rest


#### Skill Attributes

Inherit the stat of their respective Core attribute.
Players will Choose what they want their character to have, with a max of 2 (to be changed) Per core attribute
