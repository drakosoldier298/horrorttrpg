Entity ???? The Shadow

This entity has been percived only a handfull of times, Stalking people and driving them insane

It hides in somones shadow whispering to them and trying to get them to go insane lowering their sanity untill it can consume their mind, Once their mind is gone they will worm their way in and act as the host. Mimicing their behavior with slight oddities trying to infect another with it.
