# "Safety"


Entity ????? "Safety"

This entity is known as "safety" Because it mimics safe zones, Such as lockers or the doors to rooms that should hold weapons or food.

The entity will make sounds like stomache growl, and wimper the closer you get to it

The wimpering observed ranges from The sounds of an injured child to the sounds of an injured puppy, Seeming to try and play on a persons Empathy to get them closer.

# "Combat"
There is no real combat with this creature Just avoid it.
